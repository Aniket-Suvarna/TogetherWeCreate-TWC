FAQ

Q. How to become a TWC member?
Ans.You just have to click the Register/ Sign-in button on the top right. You need to enter your details and then click on Register.
Q. I am a donor. What are benefits of validation to me as a donor?
Ans.Eligibility and preference for fundraising through TWC. It is a great learning exercise for the NGO, as interaction with TWC Team helps gain insight into various aspects of NGO management and accountability. Helps root out malpractices in the organization and ensures a system of transparency. Identifies areas for improvement, which can be strengthened through capacity building training & workshops.
Q. What is done after Donation?
Ans. After donation, a letter with certificate of validation is issued to the NGO which remains valid for the 1 year after which revalidation of NGOs is required. The validated NGOs remain in the database of TWC validated charities and will be contacted upon by TWC depending upon the locational & programmatic priorities of a particular donor.
Q. What are the benefits of being a TWC member ?
Ans. Easy and simple identification & selection of credible & competent NGO partners Eliminating the need for researching reliable NGO partners by donors themselves, thus saving both time & resources
