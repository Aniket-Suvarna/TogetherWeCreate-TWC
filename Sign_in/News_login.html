CamelBak: DitchDisposable Pledge

Tap water is far more cost-efficient and earth-friendly than bottled water. According to the watchdog group Food & Water Watch, bottled water is on average 2,000 times more expensive than tap water. ...
Bottled water also damages the environment far more than tap water. In 2016, the United States used 4 billion pounds of plastic for bottled water production. That equates to 54 million barrels of oil. One person going disposable-free for a month saves an average of 18 bottles from entering landfills. Purchasing your own reusable bottle and tossing it in a work or gym bag takes no time. Best of all, it’s free to fill up.
